UAV

An unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), commomly known as a drone, is an aircraft without human pilot aborad.

UAVs are a component of an unmanned aircraft system (UAS); which include a UAV, a ground-based controller, and a system of communication between the two. The flight of UAVs may operate with various degree of autonomy: either under remote control by a human operate or autonomously by conboard computers.
